Telegram Bot
Connect nclaw to Telegram to interact with your agent from anywhere on your phone. Uses long polling — no public server or domain name required.
1
Open Telegram and search for @BotFather, then start a chat with it.
2
Send the /newbot command to create your bot.
/newbot
3
Choose a display name (e.g. My nclaw) and then a unique username ending in _bot.
4
BotFather sends you a bot token. It looks like:
1234567890:ABCdefGHIjklMNOpqrSTUvwxYZ
5
Paste the token in the field below and click Save Token. The bot starts immediately — no restart needed.
6
Search for your bot in Telegram and send /start. You're connected!

Allowed Users (Whitelist)
How to find your Telegram User ID:
Send /myid to your bot in Telegram — it will reply with your numeric ID and @username.
Paste that ID or username below, then click Save Whitelist.

Leave empty to allow anyone who knows the bot. Comma-separate multiple entries.
